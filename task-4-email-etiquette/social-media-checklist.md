# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do's

- Use strong and secure passwords for all social media accounts.
- Think before posting to maintain a positive digital reputation.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely online.
- Adjust privacy settings to protect personal information.

## ❌ Don'ts

- Do not share personal details such as address, phone number, or passwords.
- Do not post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
- Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people.
- Do not click suspicious links from unknown sources.
- Avoid oversharing your daily location or sensitive activities online.
